Food

Paris is famous for its amazing food like the croissants a cheap all butter bread usually eaten for breakfast. It is also recognised for its massive production of cheese, pasta and quality wine. Its not just rumours but they say Paris street food is the best, they call it Jambon-beurre.The resturants and and market stalls make the best lasagne and its said to have the best onion soup.
